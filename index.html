How squads are calculated:

Each squad consists of 7 of your entered pals and is evaluated under four criteria: highest total Attack, highest total Defense, highest total Power, and the best overall sum of all stats.

All pal stats are added together. Role bonuses apply ‚Äî Tanks gain +20% Defense, DPS gain +30% Attack.
